Our Mission

Breaking barriers and building futures in sports.

From Streets to Stadiums

We believe talent is universal, but opportunity is not. Millions of gifted individuals, especially in underserved communities, never get the chance to showcase their skills due to a lack of resources, guidance, and exposure. SportSpark AI was born from a desire to change that.

Our goal is to put a world-class coach, a personal nutritionist, and a talent scout right in the palm of your hand. By leveraging the power of Artificial Intelligence, we provide objective, data-driven feedback to help you improve, stay healthy, and get noticed—all for free.

A Simple Path to Success

Three easy steps to start your professional journey.

1. Record & Upload

Use your phone to record your performance—a practice drill, a workout, or a game moment. Upload it securely to our platform.

2. Get AI Analysis

Our advanced AI analyzes your technique, speed, and form. Receive a detailed report with actionable insights and personalized drills to improve.

3. Grow & Connect

Chat with our AI Coach for health and nutrition tips. As you progress, we help connect you with scouts and opportunities to take the next step.
